Airtap is an app for voice chats that bridges the gap between deep work and collaboration

Here are a few reasons to choose Airtap

Discreet & customizable

You can schedule collaborative office hours for when you feel chatty or focused hours for when you need to perform at your highest level.

Respects your time

You can stop feeling pressured to react to chat notifications.

Eliminates meetings

You can have instant conversations with anyone. You don’t have to jump into meetings that interrupt your work.

Puts privacy first

You are always muted by default, but you can push-to-talk whenever you feel like it.

Fun & collaborative

You can hang out in voice rooms with your friends after work.

Improves onboarding

You can check in on new remote hires easily, and new hires can ask questions with less friction and hesitation.
